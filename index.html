About

NYU's ACM-W is the first student chapter at the Tandon School of Engineering to focus on celebrating and giving opportunities to women in computing machinery.

Our past meetings included workshops on designing web portfolios, creating projects with GitHub, and coding on Raspberry Pi.

Connect with us on our Facebook page to stay up to date with future events!

Events

General Meetings

Our meetings and events often occur bi-weekly. These include general discussion meetings, workshops, social outings, game nights, and fundraisers.
